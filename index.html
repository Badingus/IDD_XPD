BACKSTORY

Christian von Koenigsegg founded Koenigsegg in Sweden in 1994 to create the ultimate sports car. Starting in a small studio, the company released its first prototype, the CC, in 1996. Known for hypercars like the Agera, Jesko, and Regera, Koenigsegg has set records in speed and efficiency. Despite its size, the brand excels through innovation, pioneering lightweight materials and hybrid technologies to lead the hypercar industry.
